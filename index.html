Operational Excellence

Our commitment to safety, ethics, and the community.

Safety First

We maintain a "Zero Harm" policy. Every worker is provided with proper PPE and mandatory safety training before entering any site in Nimba County.

Ethical Partnerships

In accordance with our 25% mine-owner equity model, we treat local landowners as partners, ensuring fair compensation and transparent communication.

Environmental Care

Mining and agriculture must coexist. We implement land reclamation projects to ensure that mining areas are restored for future cocoa or sugarcane use.

Zero Tolerance

We strictly prohibit child labor and forced labor, adhering to the Laws of Liberia and international human rights standards.

Land Use & Stakeholder Disclosure

Compliance Statement: Goshen Group of Companies operates in strict accordance with the Liberian Land Rights Act of 2018. We recognize that land in Nimba County is often held under Customary Ownership.

Stakeholder Consent

No mining or agricultural project is initiated without "Free, Prior, and Informed Consent" (FPIC) from the local community elders and land committees.

Environmental Mitigation

As per Article II of our incorporation, our sugarcane and cocoa operations are designed to rehabilitate soil following mineral extraction cycles.
